Benefits for local artists

Gain more exposure on Spotify.

The newly redesigned Spotify will promote local indie artists throughout the app and will help you get more diverse listeners, rapid music feedback and data analytics on user interaction with your profile. With new features like local indie playlists, we will help you break out and test your music in your local community and then help you promote it to larger international communities.

Build your Brand and Music Resume

We collate your music and suggest and connect you with similar artists and music in your genre. Our resources help you create graphics for your music and albums and set you up with your online music resume. We create an online resume using data analytics on your followers, music listens, playlist and concerts that can be easily shared with all connected venues and musical partners in your community and around the world.

Connect and Share your music with fans, artists and venues.

Local artists currently usually play songs in local venues and sell CDs but do not keep a long term communication with their listeners. This would ensure that local artists would get more daily listeners. .

Features for Spotify Listeners

Discover new local indie music

Spotify will provide a wider selection of local indie music compared to all other music platforms. The discover feature will recommend indie music to you based on your music taste.

Explore music events near you

You will be able to directly interact with artists and meet people having the same music tastes in these events.

Support artists from the beginning

You will be able to learn more about their artists by getting an exclusive behind the scenes experience.
